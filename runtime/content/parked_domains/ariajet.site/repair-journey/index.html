Current project Diagnostics

Building a repeatable intake checklist

I am turning each repair request into the same starting process: phone model, symptoms, damage history, charge test, buttons, cameras, speakers, microphones, storage, and software status.

Lesson learned Cleaning

Not every charging issue needs a new part

Lint and debris can make a phone look like it has a bad charging port. Cleaning first is safer, cheaper, and more honest than jumping straight to parts.

Completed repair Restoration

Charging port cleanup and function check

Removed debris from a charging port, cleaned the surrounding area, tested cable fit, and confirmed charging behavior after the cleanup.

Completed repair Device cleaning

Speaker grille cleaning and exterior refresh

Cleaned buildup from the speaker area and device edges, then checked playback to confirm the sound path was clearer.

Practice bench Screen repair

Practicing safe screen replacement steps

I am practicing heat control, opening tools, adhesive cleanup, flex cable handling, and post-repair display testing before expanding screen jobs.

Next skill Battery work

Battery replacement safety checklist
