Current focus

Build a reliable diagnostic checklist

Before quoting parts, I am practicing a repeatable intake process: symptoms, model, damage history, charging test, button test, camera check, speaker check, and software notes.

Lesson learned

Clean ports before assuming parts failed

Some charging issues come from packed lint or dirt. A careful cleaning can save a customer from buying parts they do not need.
