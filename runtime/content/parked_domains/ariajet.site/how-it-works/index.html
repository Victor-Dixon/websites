How it works

A faster, calmer way to start a private flight request.

AriaJet guides travelers through the minimum useful information, turns it into a structured mission profile, and supports the handoff with concierge-grade clarity.

Start Flight Request Explore Platform
Three-step flow

Simple for the traveler, structured for the team.

Step 1

Submit the travel mission

Share departure, destination, timing, passenger count, and the service details that affect aircraft fit or concierge planning.

INPUT=route,timing,passengers,luggage,preferences
Step 2

AI shapes the flight brief

The request is organized into a concise mission profile so a concierge can understand the need without chasing basic details.

OUTPUT=mission_profile,next_questions,priority_notes
Step 3

Concierge confirms next steps

Human review handles nuance: aircraft fit, timing sensitivity, traveler preferences, ground logistics, and follow-up communication.

HANDOFF=concierge_review,option_discussion,coordination
What to prepare

The details that make a flight brief useful.

Private aviation moves faster when the request includes mission-critical context from the start.

Open Form
Route

Origin, destination, and timing

Airport codes are helpful, but city names work for the first pass. Include date flexibility when possible.

Travelers

Passengers, pets, and luggage

Passenger count, baggage volume, pets, and special handling needs shape the aircraft conversation.

Experience

Concierge and service preferences

Ground transport, catering, privacy needs, and arrival expectations help create a premium journey.
