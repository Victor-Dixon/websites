## Problem

Most traders already have charts, broker history, and screenshots.

That is not the problem.

The problem is that the story is scattered:

- broker fills
- chart movement
- screenshots
- thesis notes
- emotional decisions
- exits
- missed continuations
- repeated mistakes

A normal journal records what happened.

FreeRideInvestor shows what your behavior repeated.

6. Turn the lesson into a rule.
