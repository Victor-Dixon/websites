The Protocol

Spark Protocol is built around deterministic mechanics. The rules engine separates calculable backend logic from cinematic narration.

Core systems

  • Domain scoring: maps answers into core power domains.
  • Flavor scoring: selects sub-affinities and manifested powers.
  • Combat Capability: reads power sheets into a stable strength score.
  • The Exchange: spends spare capacity on upgrades without breaking the cap.
  • Missions: stages saved Sparks into rescue, investigation, and containment paths.
  • Battle Simulator: applies arena, odds, and resolution rules.

The goal is not random power fantasy. The goal is fair, repeatable, explainable outcomes.
