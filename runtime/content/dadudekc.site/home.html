Spark Protocol

A deterministic fantasy-superhero rules engine for character generation, power scaling, combat capability, exchange upgrades, and fair battle simulation.

The promise is simple: cinematic outcomes on the surface, auditable mechanics underneath.

What Spark Protocol does

  • Generates characters from domain and flavor scoring.
  • Maps powers into tiers, combat capability, and threat classes.
  • Uses deterministic rules for upgrades through the Exchange.
  • Supports fair matchup logic for fan-favorite battle questions.
