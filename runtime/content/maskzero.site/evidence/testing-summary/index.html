Test methodology

Methodology should document organism or surrogate selection, aerosol or airflow conditions, chamber geometry, UV-C wavelength, irradiance, dwell time, dose calculation, controls, replication, and pass/fail criteria.

Performance language

Public claims use "claimed / evidence-linked" wording until final reports support specific pathogen reduction, neutralization, filtration, reuse, and source-control statements.

Safety verification

Safety testing should verify UV-C containment, user exposure limits, electrical safety, materials, fit, airflow resistance, thermal behavior, cleaning, and instructions for use.
