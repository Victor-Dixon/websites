The Emergence

The Emergence began as a machine for answering “Who would win?” and evolved into a world where the answer could include you.

This is the controlled client preview for the Spark Protocol / Emergence website direction. The full product pages are staged as drafts until review.

Product direction

The Emergence is a deterministic fantasy-superhero system. It starts with fair matchup logic, then expands into character generation, power scaling, upgrade rules, and battle simulation.

The surface can feel cinematic. The backend stays auditable.

Core promise

  • Answer fan-favorite matchup questions fairly.
  • Let users generate themselves as fantasy superheroes.
  • Resolve powers through deterministic mechanics instead of vague hand-waving.
  • Show story outcomes without exposing every backend calculation.

Preview sections prepared

  • The Protocol: rules credibility page draft.

  • Battle Simulator: matchup demo placeholder draft.

Review questions

  • Should the first public demo be character generation or battle simulation?
  • Should the site lead with “Who would win?” or “Become part of the world?”
  • Should visitors see rules-engine proof, or mostly cinematic outcomes with trust markers?
  • What should the first paid/client-facing offer be?
