The Promise

Fair underneath. Cinematic on top.

Spark Protocol exists to answer power questions without hand-waving. The player sees story. The system keeps the receipts.

Protocol Rules

  • Abilities have limits, costs, counters, and environments where they shine.
  • Arena conditions matter: weather, distance, terrain, visibility, and civilians can flip outcomes.
  • The result is resolved before narration. The story never gets to cheat the engine.
  • Close fights feel messy. Stomps stay honest. Upsets require traceable reasons.
