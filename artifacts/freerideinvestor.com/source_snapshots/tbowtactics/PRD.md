# TBOWTactics Product Requirements Document

## Overview
TBOWTactics is an iOS application that helps small-account traders research stocks, visualize trends, generate AI-assisted trade plans, and configure alerts. The goal is to provide an approachable toolset that elevates trading discipline and decision making.

## Goals
- Deliver a real-time stock research dashboard with live market data.
- Present 30-day historical charts to highlight trends and patterns.
- Offer AI-powered trade plans with entries, exits, and risk tips.
- Allow users to create and manage local price alerts.

## Non-Goals
- Cross-platform support beyond iOS.
- Server-side alert infrastructure.
- Advanced analytics or portfolio management.

## User Stories (MVP)
1. As a trader, I can enter a stock symbol and view current price, daily change, and sentiment so I can act quickly.
2. As a trader, I can inspect a 30-day historical chart to evaluate recent performance.
3. As a trader, I can request an AI-generated trade plan outlining potential entries, exits, and risk management.
4. As a trader, I can set price-based alerts and receive local notifications when conditions are met.

## Success Metrics
- Successful API calls to Alpha Vantage and OpenAI.
- Daily active users and retention over time.
- User feedback indicating improved trading confidence.

## Risks & Assumptions
- Reliance on third-party APIs for data and AI capabilities.
- Users must provide valid API keys.
- Network connectivity is required for most features.

## Milestones
- **MVP Completion:** core research dashboard, historical charts, AI trade plans, and local alerts.
- **Beta Release:** gather feedback from a small test group.
- **Public Launch:** open the app for broader use once stability and usability goals are met.
