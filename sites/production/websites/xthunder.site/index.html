Storm Siege — Build & Fight

  1. Build phase: Click the grid to place structures. Spend thunder coins wisely.
  2. Walls block enemies. Bolt Towers shoot. Storm Halls spawn knights when you fight.
  3. Shock Traps zap the first enemy that steps on them. You can arm up to 10 shock traps per round.
  4. Hit Strike — Fight! to unleash a wave. Between waves you can keep building.
  5. Boss waves arrive every 5 rounds (5 & 10). After clearing the wave before a boss, the game pauses for prep — organize, then hit Face the Boss!
  6. Protect your Thunder Core (glowing center). If it falls, you lose — hit Restart Game to try again.
  7. Survive 10 waves to win. Earn coins for kills and wave clears.
