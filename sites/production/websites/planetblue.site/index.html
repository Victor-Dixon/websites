World Systems

  • Walkable overworld — explore zones on foot; tap tiles to pathfind.
  • Shining Force combat — tap-to-move and attack with move/attack range highlights.
  • Zone influence — victories push safety; defeats raise threat across the map.
  • Nemesis foes — survivors gain names, titles, and resistances; they return.
  • Morality axis — good and evil choices tint the world and unlock paths.
