Alcohol Policy

Host provides alcohol.

Houston Sip Queen provides mobile bartending service, setup support, drink menu planning, mixer and garnish guidance, and event service. The host is responsible for purchasing and providing alcohol for the event unless a separate licensed arrangement is confirmed.

  • We do not sell alcohol directly through this website.
  • Service is reviewed before booking.
  • Guests must be of legal drinking age to consume alcohol.
  • We reserve the right to refuse unsafe service.
