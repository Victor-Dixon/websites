Repair Limits

Clear work. No guessing.

Every request is reviewed first. If the job is outside our current skill level, we say no instead of risking the device.

Currently taking

  • Screen protector installs
  • Phone case installs
  • Basic phone cleaning
  • Charging-port cleaning
  • Simple screen replacement requests
  • Basic diagnostics

Not currently taking

  • Water damage
  • Motherboard repair
  • Locked or iCloud-locked phones
  • Severe frame damage
  • Data recovery
  • Repairs without review first
