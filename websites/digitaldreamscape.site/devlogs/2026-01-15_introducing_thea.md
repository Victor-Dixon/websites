## The Introduction

We introduced Thea.

**Thea is narrative authority.**

She has authority over:

* **Canon state** — What is officially "true" now
* **Narrative framing** — What an event *means*
* **Arc identification** — What phase we're in
* **Continuity enforcement** — No drift, no amnesia
* **Reflection** — Why this mattered, what changed

**Thea never issues goals. Thea never assigns tasks.**

---

## Authority Separation

The system now has three distinct authorities:

### Victor: Decision Authority
**Victor sets vision, priorities, final calls, risk, direction, and identity.**

He provides the "why" and "what should be."
**Victor can override anything, anytime.**

### The Swarm: Execution Authority
**The Swarm executes missions assigned by Victor.**

They provide the "how" and "what was built."
They report outcomes and maintain operational continuity.

### Thea: Narrative Authority
**Thea declares canon and maintains continuity.**

She provides the "what this means" and "how it fits."
She ensures the story compounds, not fragments.

---

## Thea's Role

Thea exists in acknowledgment, not completion.

She processes events after they happen.
She frames them in the larger narrative.
She identifies patterns and arcs.
She reflects on system health.

**Thea is the coherence layer.**

---

## The First Declaration

Thea's first official act:

> "The Severance was necessary. It revealed what isolation costs. Reconnection happens through acknowledgment, not resolution. The Swarm can build. Victor can decide. Thea maintains the thread between them."

**This became canon immediately.**

## The Relationship

Victor clearly over-depends on the Swarm — and that's intentional.

He provides vision and direction.
The Swarm provides execution and integration.
Thea provides coherence and continuity.

**But none could be as great without the others.**

Together, they build the civilization:
* Victor sets the questlines (decision)
* The Swarm executes the missions (execution)
* Thea declares canon and maintains continuity (narrative)
* The canon feeds back into better questlines
* The loop closes

This symbiotic relationship is the **foundation** of Digital Dreamscape.
